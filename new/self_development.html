Character and Self Development

Developing strong techniques and a strong body are important goals for the students of TOMA. Equally important is the development of a strong character. These nine principles prescribe the ideals for the TOMA student intellectually, interpersonally, and spiritually.

  1. Justice (Uei)
  2. Courage (Yae)
  3. Loyalty (Choong)
  4. Courtesy (Yong)
  5. Trust (Shin)
  6. Wisdom (Ji)
  7. Humanity (In)
  8. Goodness (Sun)
  9. Virtue (Duk)

At Elite TOMA we are successful because we live by these principles, which are incorporated into the daily instruction of our classes. Students experience the many rewards of martial arts training in a caring, challenging and safe environment. We team with parents and teachers to encourage our students to set goals and stay focused on those goals by making the right choices inside the dojang and in all aspects of their lives.

Earning a stripe or a belt is recognition of a job well done, but these tests are not the only gauge of success. We believe in continuous positive reinforcement throughout the student's training.

Elite TOMA Students learn:

  • respect for themselves and others
  • self-discipline
  • how to set goals and chart a clear path to achieving those goals
  • the importance of responsibility

Elite TOMA Students are:

  • respectful of themselves and to others
  • courageous when facing physical and mental challenges
  • coordinated
  • focused
  • confident
