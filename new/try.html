Elite T O M A

Traditional

Techniques and External Arts: Kicking, punching, self-defense, weapons training and other types of "hard" Martial Arts train our bodies and contributes to good health.

Oriental

Obedience and Respect: All of our training ultimately focuses back on respect, love and caring for our families, our neighbors and our country. A true martial artist uses their skills for the good of all, to help build better families, friendships, community, country and, ultimately, a better world.

Martial

Meditation and Internal Arts: Meditation, "Ki" (or energy) cultivation, and "soft" arts help us train both our bodies and our minds and lead to longevity and self-control.

Arts

Ancestry and Culture: Learning both the history and culture of Korea and other Oriental countries gives us a firm foundation to help us better understand why we train the way we do, and the history behind many of the techniques and forms we practice.
