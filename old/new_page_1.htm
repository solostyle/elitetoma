Tai Chi once an art form practiced by only a handful of students, is now practiced all over the world.  Tai Chi incorporates techniques from several martial arts styles including deep breathing and meditation exercises of Qi Gong to clear the mind.

When the breathing and meditation exercises of Qi Gong are combined with the physical movements of Tai Chi, the product is a martial art style like no other in the entire world.  It is often referred to as "meditation in motion".  The smooth, graceful movements of Tai Chi have attracted students from all walks of life.  All ages can benefit from this training:

the athlete to center himself.
the working professional to reduce stress and anxiety.
the golden age person to maintain youthfulness and vigor.
the very active person with no time, patience, or energy, yet still striving for more.

Whether you are seeking an exercise program, a way to better health, or a way to relax and find you center, Tai Chi and Qi Gong can help you achieve your goal.
