 At Elite TOMA we are successful because we live by a set  of principles that are incorporated into the daily instruction of our classes.  Children experience the many rewards of martial arts training in a caring, challenging and safe environment.  We team with parents and teachers to encourage our students to set goals and stay focused on those goals by making the right choices inside the dojang and in all aspects of their lives.

Earning a stripe or a belt is recognition of a job well done, but these tests are not the only gauge of success.  We believe in a continuous positive reinforcement throughout the students' training.

Elite TOMA Students learn:
respect for themselves and others
self-discipline
how to set goals and chart a clear path to achieving those goals
the importance of responsibility

Elite TOMA Students are:

respectful of themselves and to others
courageous when facing physical and mental challenges
coordinated
focused
self-confident
