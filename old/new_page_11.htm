Welcome to Elite TOMA

Elite TOMA is recognized and certified by the World Tae Kwon Do (WTF) and the International Hap Ki Do Federation (IHF).

Separate curriculums for children and adults are offered in several martial arts disciplines: Tae Kwon Do, Hap Ki Do, Tai Chi and Qi Gong.  Students are introduced to weapons training when it is appropriate to their rank and skill level.

Training in martial arts is personal to each student.  Everyone progresses at their own pace.  As a student, you are looked upon as an individual, never compared to anyone else.

Helping our young students stay focused and goal oriented is a top priority at Elite TOMA.  We work with parents and teachers to encourage students to stay focused on their goals by making the right choices not only inside the Do Jang but in every aspect of their lives.

 Elite TOMA offers a variety of events, including TOMA Festivals, Break-A-Thons, Ski Trips, White Water Rafting, Adult TOMA Retreats, Summer Day Camp, Summer Picnic, Holiday Party, Bring a Buddy Week and Open House.

Also offered are Parent Appreciation Nights, giving parents a few hours to relax as thanks to the parents for their support of our students and our school; and Children's Sleepover Night, an expanded version of Parent Appreciation Night.

Check our Calendar for upcoming events and dates as well as our class times.
